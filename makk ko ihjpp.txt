<section id="threats" class="py-5">
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="text-center mb-4">⚠️ Threats to Marine Species</h2>
    <p class="lead text-center">
      Marine ecosystems face increasing threats due to human activities and environmental changes. Understanding these threats is crucial to protecting the health of our oceans.
    </p>
    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-md-6">
        <h4>🌍 Climate Change</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Rising sea temperatures lead to coral bleaching and habitat loss.</li>
          <li>Melting ice caps impact polar marine life and ocean currents.</li>
          <li>Ocean acidification weakens shells of invertebrates and coral reefs.</li>
        </ul>

        <h4>🐠 Overfishing</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Depletes fish populations and disrupts the food web.</li>
          <li>Bycatch harms non-target species like dolphins and turtles.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="col-md-6">
        <h4>🗑 Pollution</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Plastic waste entangles animals and pollutes habitats.</li>
          <li>Oil spills and chemical runoff damage marine ecosystems.</li>
        </ul>

        <h4>🏗 Habitat Destruction</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Coastal development and dredging destroy coral reefs and mangroves.</li>
          <li>Bottom trawling damages the ocean floor and sensitive species.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section id="conservation" class="py-5 bg-light">

    <h2 class="text-center mb-4">🌱 Conservation of Marine Species</h2>
    <p class="lead text-center">
      Conservation efforts are essential to protect marine species and ensure the long-term sustainability of our oceans.
      Through responsible actions, policies, and awareness, we can help restore balance and biodiversity in marine ecosystems.
    </p>
    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-md-6">
        <h4>🔬 Marine Protected Areas (MPAs)</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Designated zones where marine life is protected from human activity.</li>
          <li>Help restore fish populations and biodiversity.</li>
        </ul>

        <h4>🧼 Pollution Reduction</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Reducing single-use plastics and encouraging recycling.</li>
          <li>Proper disposal of waste to prevent ocean pollution.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="col-md-6">
        <h4>🐟 Sustainable Fishing</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Using methods that minimize bycatch and allow fish stocks to recover.</li>
          <li>Supporting eco-certified seafood products.</li>
        </ul>

        <h4>📚 Education & Awareness</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Promoting marine conservation through schools and communities.</li>
          <li>Encouraging public involvement in clean-up campaigns and citizen science.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
